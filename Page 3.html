Defense

The objective of the defense is to prevent the other team from scoring, and they do so by forcing the offense to turn the ball over either by preventing them from achieving a first down and forcing a punt, forcing the offense to fumble or throw an interception, or more rarely, forcing a turnover on downs.
The defense is made up of 3 groups:

  • Defensive line
  • Linebackers
  • Defensive Backs
