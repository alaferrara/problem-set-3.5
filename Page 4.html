Special Teams


Special teams is the units on the field during kicking plays such as punts, kick returns, kickoffs, extra points, and field goals.
The different positions include:

  • Kicker
  • Holder
  • Long Snapper
  • Punter
  • Kickoff Specialist
  • Kick returner
  • Punt returner
  • Upback
  • Gunner
  • Jammer
